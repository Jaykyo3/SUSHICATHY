Executive Summary

🎯 Business Concept

Sushi Catch combines fresh, affordable sushi with an interactive fishing game where customers can win sushi prizes. Customers may buy sushi directly or participate in the game for an added fun experience.

💡 Unique Value

We don't just sell sushi - we sell an experience. The combination of food and gaming creates memorable interactions that set us apart from traditional food stalls.

🎮 Dual Revenue Streams

• Direct sushi sales (₱30-₱40 per order)
• Game participation (₱30 per play)
• Multiple ways for customers to engage
