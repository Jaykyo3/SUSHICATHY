Our Complete Business Strategy

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

🎯 Business Concept

Sushi Catch combines fresh, affordable sushi with an interactive fishing game where customers can win sushi prizes. Customers may buy sushi directly or participate in the game for an added fun experience.

💰 Financial Highlights

  • Startup Capital: ₱3,150 (5 members × ₱630 each)
  • Daily Sales Target: 30+ sushi orders
  • Break-even Point: 2 months
  • Monthly Profit Projection: ₱11,000

MARKET ANALYSIS

🎯 Target Market

Grade 7-12 students and school staff at Don Bosco High School Parañaque with daily allowances of ₱50-₱100.

📊 Market Justification

Students prefer affordable food options (₱30-₱45 range) that are both filling and trendy. Sushi is popular and rarely sold in school-based businesses.

💡 Value Proposition

We don't just sell sushi - we sell an experience. Customers can choose between buying directly or enjoying a fun fishing game for their food.

PESTLE Analysis

📊 Political

Following school safety and cleanliness regulations

💵 Economic

Students have daily allowances (₱50-₱100) perfect for our pricing

👥 Social

Japanese food trends and fun activities appeal to students

📱 Technological

Using social media (FB groups, Messenger) for promotion

⚖️ Legal

Compliant with all school business guidelines

🌱 Environmental

Using eco-friendly packaging to minimize waste

SWOT ANALYSIS

💪 Strengths

  • Unique concept combining food and fun
  • Affordable pricing
  • Reusable materials
  • Minimal new expenses

⚠️ Weaknesses

  • Limited working capital
  • Perishable ingredients require careful storage

🚀 Opportunities

  • High student curiosity about interactive food stalls
  • Potential for expansion through themed sushi days
  • Growing popularity of Japanese cuisine

🌪️ Threats

  • Competing food stalls
  • Food spoilage
  • Inconsistent demand during exams or breaks
